About

I am a self employed UX Designer & Product Strategist. Freelancing and starting my own efforts here and there.

Currently living somewhere between Vienna - Linz - Munich.

Bio

Born and raised on the cosy upper Austrian country side, together with plenty of family time in the big city of (West) Berlin. Moved to Vienna at the age of 18, university time...deeply fallen in love with that city and with "design". Started young at an upcoming "web 2.0" agency with an internship. Grown with that company over the years. Had hunger for more. Moved to Berlin for 2 years, soaked up lots of city live there and created an iPhone App startup as homage to "Neukölln". Moved further north to live in Copenhagen for a year and to study at the amazing and truly life changing Copenhagen Institute of Interaction Design.

Founded another startup right out of university, which I brought back to Vienna and developed till 2015. Always had an professionell interest in accessiblity over the years and founded an open source project, right before Google ringed to bell - Signed up and moved to Munich for ~5 years, while being up in the air to co-work with many amazing & smart Google collegues on UI-design standards, accessiblity and account setting projects across the Google universe (including many month in Silicon Valley and Tel Aviv). Felt the desire to move "home" and came back to Upper Austria & Vienna to found my own studio... to be continued
