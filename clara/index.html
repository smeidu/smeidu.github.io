Clara

platform for caregiving relatives

UX Lead & Co-PO (2020 - ongoing)

Currently in active development.

www.alles-clara.at

Product vision

Around one million people in Austria look after family members or other people close to them. They provide support, organize visits to the doctor, help with eating or are simply there and listen.

Clara provides professional advisers to those caregiving relatives. In virtual consultation rooms they are in direct and personal contact. It is about current issues as well as long-term support.

Product strategy & management

I helped to establish the initial vision and to push things forward to the first alpha und beta releases in my role as Co-Productowner & UX Lead: defining vision and roadmap; explore and define features, organise our team's work in meetings and on Miro; co-work with developers to translate mocks and flows to code and interactivity; find, test & fix those bugs, organise - run - analyse user tests;

Shape the product: Harness insights from customers, stakeholders, and data to prioritize and build a product that will have the most impact on the business. Ship the product: Ship high-quality product on time and free of surprises. Synchronize the people: Align all stakeholders around one vision, strategy, goal, roadmap, and timeline to avoid wasted time and effort.

Clara design system

is based on the Material React UI library and it's excellent Figma template. We defined the Clara-Theme to be tailored for an broad, eldery user base: clear contrast, large font sizes, simple UI building blocks, warm colors that also help to seperate between the different user types (caregiving users, dependent users, consultant users).

Clara UX

The platform and apps are currently in active development. We recently finished the initial alpha versions for the client app (Web / iOS / Android app for the caregiving relatives) and consultant app (Web UI for professionell consultation work). The alpha version was meant for small scale user tests and to learn and further develop the product. We are now gearing towards a bete release for scaled up test with invited users in 2022.
