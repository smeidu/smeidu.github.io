Clara

A Platform for Caregiving Relatives

UX Lead and Co-Product Owner (2020 – 2022)

Currently in active development.

www.alles-clara.at

Product Vision

Around 1 million people in Austria look after family members or other people close to them. They provide support, organize visits to the doctor, help with eating, or are simply present.

However, this often leaves caregivers with more questions than answers. So Alles Clara provides professional advisers and online counselors to help answer questions and give recommendations and assistance.

Product Strategy and Management

I helped establish the initial vision of the app and to push things forward in my role as Co-Product Owner by:

  • Defining the vision and roadmap
  • Exploring and defining features
  • Organizing our team's work in meetings, on Miro, and on Slack
  • Working with developers to translate mockups and flows to code and interactivity
  • Testing, finding, and helping fix bugs
  • Organizing, running, and analyzing user tests

In 2022, after 15 months, Alles Clara had grown from a small "startup" setup to a proper full stack company effort. As a result, I handed over product owner duties to an FTE to fully concentrate on UX.

Design System

The Alles Clara design system is based on the Material React UI library and its excellent Figma template. We defined the theme to be tailored to a broad, elderly user base, which meant: clear contrast, large font sizes, simple UI building blocks, and warm colors that also help differentiate the various user types (caregiving users, dependent users, consultant users).

UX

The platform and apps are currently in active development. We recently finished the initial alpha versions for the client app (Web / iOS / Android app for caregiving relatives) and consultant app (Web UI for professional consultation work). The alpha version was meant for small-scale user tests and to learn and further develop the product. We're now gearing toward a beta release with invited users in 2022.

Crew

The technical side is driven by the Semantic Labs team. I was brought on during the initial research phase to help with design thinking, exploration, and the initial product definition. Once "Clara" was born as a proper vision, we moved forward and started working on the MVP.
